Q2 Proposal
⚠

The Problem

Dual workforce under stress. 33.37% attrition. Gig workers have zero mental health access. Post-IPO survivor guilt at HQ.

🛡

What We Offer

24/7 anonymous counselling in local languages. HR burnout dashboard. Manager program. Gig welfare module under new Labour Code.

🕐

Why Now

India's Code on Social Security mandates 1 to 2% of annual turnover on gig welfare. That budget must be spent by law.

🎯

What Success Looks Like

3 months: 35% adoption. 6 months: first HR heatmap. 12 months: CHRO rollout, Swiggy becomes a case study.

Industry benchmark: 4x ROI on mental health investment
Q3 Upsell

Upsell 1: Manager Enablement Program

Train managers to spot early stress signals and refer employees to counselling before things escalate. Sits under L&D budget, not just wellness.

"What if your managers caught burnout 3 weeks before it hit performance?"

Upsell 2: Wellness Intelligence Dashboard

Live department-level stress heatmaps. Shifts YourDOST from vendor to strategic partner. Hard to churn once HR presents it to the board.

"You have performance data. You have engagement data. This is the missing layer."
